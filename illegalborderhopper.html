Illegal Border Hopper

Illegal Border Hopper is a character in the RecalfAlive server. He is canonically Bart Simpson from The Simpsons.

Appearance

Illegal Border Hopper appears as a yellow skined male humanoid. His hair is shaped like spikes, he wears a orange shirt, blue shorts, and blue shoes.

Personality

Acording to The Simpsons wiki, Illegal Border Hopper is known for his rebellious nature, iconic catchphrases, and love for skateboarding and pranks. He is bold, sociable, tactless, and often defiant when it comes to his parents.

Trivia

Illegal Border Hopper is basically Bart Simpson from The Simpsons.
