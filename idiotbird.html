Idiot Bird

Idiot Bird (Bird) is a character in the RecalfAlive server. They are a part of the Tankmen Mercenary.

Appearance

Idiot Bird appears as a 5' 5" non-binary humanoid. They wear a black armor with a white bulletproof vest, wears black gloves and black boots, and a sling for their sniper rifle. They also wear a helmet with swank goggles with only their mouth visible. Their generic appearance bears a resemblance to other tankmen, Though what makes notable them from the majority is their shorter stature and lithesome physique.

Personality

Bird is untalkative, reserved and greatly secretive, going as far as hiding their true identity. Though even they dont speak much and are quieter in general, their body language makes their mood very apparent. They are usually cautious and prefer to be independent, making hard to gain their trust but they are extremely loyal to whom they do. To top everything off, they are quite stubbornly determined, standing unwavering in the face of a threat even if their life is risked.

Abilities

Agility: Bird has some serious speed, paired with being quick off the mark they are tricky to pursue.

Bird specifically works as a sniper for the Tankmen, they have a powerful mastery of sniper rifles. They'll wipe an enemy off of the face of the earth with terrifying accuracy.

Weaknesses

Lack of Strength: In return for their swiftness and grace, they aren't very strong.

Lightweight: Being short and thin makes you weigh a lot less, easier to manhandle.

Low Alcohol Tolerance: They very much rather to stay sober, the effects hit them hard and it would be nice to think clearly at all times.
