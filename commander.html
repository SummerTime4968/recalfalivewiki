Commander

Commander is a character in the RecalfAlive server. He is the leader of the Henchmen.

Appearance

Commander appears as a 6' 6" male humanoid. He wears black armor with a white diagonal cross vest, wears black gloves and boots, and his armor has two military insignias on both shoulders. He also wears a helmet with only their mouth visible. The helmet has an arrow on the top and a straight visor with ends that are pointed up.

Affiliations

Tim (Friend)

Captain (Enemy)

Tankmen (Enemy)

Personality

Commander is usually courageous with average intelligence, having a sense of duty and usually he shows some kind of sympathy for his friends overall.

Trivia

While Commander was never named in the original series, in a tweet from JohnnyUtah, his name is revealed to just be 'Commander', though this was possibly a placeholder for something else.
