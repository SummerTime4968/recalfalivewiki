Cat

Cat is a character in the RecalfAlive server.

Appearance

Cat appears as a black cat with a white underbody. Their eye color appear to be yellow and they have long whiskers.

Personality

Cat is at times silly and goofy, this is shown when Cat accidentally switches a switch at a power plant while chasing a mouse. However, the Cat appears to possess higher sapience, as they are capable of speech and can communicate with other people.
