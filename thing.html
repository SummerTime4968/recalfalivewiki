Thing

Thing is a character in the RecalfAlive server. He is an extraterrestrial alien who originated from interstellar space, I guess.

Appearance

Thing appears as a 5' 8" dark colored alien. Although he is biologically genderless, he identifies as male. He has two short legs, two massive arms with five fingers on each hand and a stubby head with no neck. He does wear pants and shirts though they are hard to see as they camouflage with his body.

Personality

Thing is usually friendly, and calm, sometimes being chaotic and witty. They maintain a positive mood all the time even in tough situations. He cares about people and even going as far as fraternizing between enemies.

Abilities

Agility: Captain is able to run and think fast.

Trivia

Thing has a lot of friends because of his chill behavior.

Thing hosted a TV show once.

Thing originated from the ROBLOX horror game "Light Bulb."
