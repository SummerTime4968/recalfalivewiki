Steve

Steve is a character in the RecalfAlive server. He is a part of the Tankmen, and is Captain's friend.

Appearance

Steve appears as a 6' 4" male humanoid. He wears black armor with a white bulletproof vest, wears black gloves and black boots, and his armor has two military insignias on both shoulders. He also wears a helmet with goggles with only his mouth visible. His generic appearance slightly bears a resemblance to other tankmen.

Affiliations

Captain (Friend)

Commander (Enemy)

Henchmen (Enemy)

Personality

Steve gets along well with friends, especially the Henchmens. However, he remains a coward as he is a little incompetent and dim-witted.

Abilities

Agility: Steve is rather agile and cunning, these traits make them tricky to fight with

Weaknesses

Cowardness: Steve's cowardness makes him quite vulnerable to attacks, which is shown when the Thugmens attack him.

Trivia

Steve's favorite cheese is American and Mild cheddar.

Steve's design comes from the original Newgrounds logo, where the Tankman wore goggles instead of a visor, before it was redesigned by JohnnyUtah.

He's been confirmed to be 16. Just rewatch Tankmen 2.
