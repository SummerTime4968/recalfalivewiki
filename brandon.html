Brandon

Brandon is a character in the RecalfAlive server. He is dead, lol.

Appearance

Brandon appears as a 6' 4" male robot. Brandon wears a cyan colored collar shirt with an amethyst tie, has black trousers and hat, dark grey belt and black hair. His face is a "visor" with yellow eyes appearing on it and his mouth below. He also has a tail that ends in a yellow syringe filled with corrosive substance.

Personality

Brandon is humble, usually having a neutral expression and sometimes having a chaotic trait who smashes pretty much anything. Though he does care about his friends, aiding them in any way possible and has a great intelligence because well, he's a robot.

Abilities

Agility: Brandon is able to run and think fast.
