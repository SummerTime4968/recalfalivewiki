Charley

Charley is a character in the RecalfAlive server. He is a part of the Henchmen Infantry.

Appearance

Charley appears as a 5' 10" male humanoid. He wears black armor with a white diagonal cross vest, wears black gloves and boots, and his armor has two military insignias on both shoulders. He also wears a helmet that has an arrow on the top and a straight visor with only their mouth visible.

Affiliations

Brian (Friend)

Tankmen (Enemy)

Personality

Charley is usually lazy and optimistic, though pretty confident, patient and caring of friends and allies. He can also be short-tempered and somewhat sadistic.

Abilities

High Intelligence: Charley has high intelligence and can understand morse code and sign language.

Weaknesses

Laziness: Charley is pretty laid back and not really willing to do tasks, which can make him vulnerable to enemies.

Short Attention Span: Charley doesn't seem to pay attention to their surroundings and can be easily attacked by multiple people.

Exhaustion: Charley has limited stamina and can't go far without getting exhausted.

Trivia

Charley was originally a rip off version of Tim.
