Captain

Captain (Sergeant John Captain, John Captain, Tankman) is a character in the RecalfAlive server.

Appearance

Captain appears as a 6' male humanoid. He wears black armor with a white bulletproof vest, wears black gloves and black boots, and his armor has two military insignias on both shoulders. He also wears a helmet with swank goggles with only his mouth visible. Although Captain is the leader of the tankmen, His generic appearance bears a resemblance to other tankmen.

Personality

Captain is vulgar, courageous and can be (not usually) nice with an average intelligence and is brave, usually he is vicious, and cruel overall. Though he sometimes does show sympathy for his friends.

Agility: Captain is able to run and think fast.

Captain absolutely hates Brandon for his chaotic and reckless trait, going as far as killing him multiple times.
